Why Study MBBS in Vietnam

NMC and WHO have approved and authorized the Medical Curriculum in Vietnam, ensuring it meets international educational standards. The country's diverse culture, friendly locals, and status as one of the safest nations are key reasons for choosing to study MBBS in Vietnam. Universities provide excellent shared and private accommodation options tailored to the needs of international students, along with continental food at very affordable prices. Vietnam stands out as a nation offering high-quality medical education at a relatively low cost. The affordable living expenses make it an attractive option for international students on a budget. The country's medical universities have received accreditation from esteemed organizations such as MCI, FAIMER, WDOMS, and ECFMG. The MBBS program in Vietnam typically lasts 5 + 1 years, including a clinical internship. Job opportunities for MBBS graduates in Vietnam are plentiful due to the high demand for medical professionals. The cities in Vietnam are affordable and well-connected by public transportation. Many Indian restaurants offer both South and North Indian cuisine. Vietnam has become a favored destination for MBBS students not only from India but also from Nepal, Malaysia, Bangladesh, Africa, Mauritius, Moldova, the United Kingdom, Nigeria, and Pakistan.

    • Study MBBS in Vietnam: Medium of Instruction

    Most public universities in Vietnam use Vietnamese as the medium of instruction, while private universities offer English instruction for international students.

    Accommodation at Vietnam

    Finding accommodation in Vietnam is straightforward and very affordable. Both on-campus and off-campus living spaces are attractive and inexpensive. Student dormitories are available at very reasonable prices, and they are inspected by security personnel, showing that Vietnamese universities prioritize the safety of their MBBS students. You can choose between individual and shared spaces based on your convenience and budget. Transportation between accommodations and universities is frequent and very safe.

    Food in Vietnam
    Vietnam is a culturally diverse nation with excellent food and travel destinations, but the quality and standards of education are equally important. Continental food is widely available in Vietnam, including at universities and dormitories. The prices are very affordable; you can find three meals a day for just $2-3, which is quite economical.
    MEDICAL LICENSE OF VIETNAM

    The best aspect of pursuing an MBBS in Vietnam for Indian and other international students is the opportunity to obtain a medical license to practice in Vietnam. Students who wish to settle in Vietnam can practice and pursue their post-graduate studies in the country.
