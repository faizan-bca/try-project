Why Study MBBS In Armenia?

  • Rapidly Growing Economy: Armenia's growing economy provides promising opportunities for medical students.

  • Part-Time Job Opportunities: Armenia offers part-time job opportunities for students to supplement their finances.
  • Paid Internships: Medical students can apply for paid internships at hospitals affiliated with universities.
  • Friendly Environment: Armenia is known for its friendly people who are always willing to help.
  • English-Medium Instruction: English is the language of instruction in all medical universities in Armenia.

      5. Application Process:
    5. Fill out the application form provided by the university. This can often be done online through the university's official website.
