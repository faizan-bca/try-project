Anhui Medical University

Anhui Medical University (AMU) is a key public university located in Hefei, the capital city of Anhui Province, China. It has a strong emphasis on the medical sciences and related fields.

Beijing University of Chinese Medicine

Beijing University of Chinese Medicine (BUCM) is one of the premier institutions in China dedicated to the study and research of Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM). It plays a crucial role in the preservation, development, and globalization of TCM.

Capital Medical University

Capital Medical University (CMU), located in Beijing, China, is a prestigious institution known for its strong emphasis on medical sciences and health-related fields. It is one of the key municipal universities in Beijing and has made significant contributions to medical research, education, and healthcare services.

Chongqing Medical

Chongqing Medical University (CQMU), located in Chongqing, China, is a prominent institution specializing in medical education and research. It is known for its comprehensive programs in the field of medicine, including clinical medicine, biomedical sciences, and public health, among others.

Guangzhou University of Chinese Medicine

Guangzhou University of Chinese Medicine (GUCM) is one of China's oldest and most prestigious institutions specializing in Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM). Located in Guangzhou, a major city in southern China, it has been at the forefront of TCM education, research, and clinical practice since its establishment.

Fujian Medical University

Fujian Medical University (FMU), located in Fuzhou, the capital city of Fujian Province, China, is a prominent institution specializing in medical education and research. Established in 1937, the university has a long history of contributing to the healthcare system both locally and nationally.

Admission Process for MBBS in China

The admission process for MBBS in China is as follows-

  1. Apply on the official portal of the college.
  2. Fill the admission form on the online portal.
  3. Send the scanned copies of the documents.
  4. Get the offer letter and keep it safe for future use.
  5. Pay registration fees for your desired college.
  6. Apply for the visa after you have paid the college fees and received the confirmation letter.
  7. Get the visa to study in China.
  8. Book a flight to China.
  9. Fly to China and start attending your lectures.
Required Documents for MBBS in China

The student should keep the entire documents ready before taking admission. The required documents for MBBS in China is as follows--

  1. Copy of the Passport.

Eligibility Criteria for MBBS in China

The eligibility criteria for studying MBBS in China is as follows-

  1. Students should be a pass out 10 + 2 with a regular board pursuing PCM.
  2. 65-70% marks in physics, chemistry, biology, and English in class 12th.
  3. Cleared NEET with a minimum of 55-60%
  4. Students should know English (IELTS not required).
