About IRAN

Iran, officially the Islamic Country, is the easternmost country in the Middle East, bordered by the Caspian Sea to the north and the Persian Gulf and Gulf of Oman to the south. Formerly known as Persia, it shares land borders with Armenia, Turkey, Iraq, Afghanistan, Azerbaijan, Pakistan, and Turkmenistan, and sea boundaries with Kazakhstan, Russia, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman, the UAE, and Saudi Arabia. Iran holds a significant geopolitical position in Asia and is a founding member of the United Nations, World Economic Organization, Organization of Islamic Cooperation, and OPEC. It boasts the world's second-largest natural gas supply and fourth-largest proven oil reserves. Additionally, Iran's 22 UNESCO World Heritage Sites highlight its rich cultural legacy. ... Students from around the world choose Iran for their medical studies, and the graduates from Iranian universities are well-regarded in clinical fields worldwide. The extensive training and services provided in Iran contribute significantly to the students' career development, ensuring a fruitful and impactful professional journey. While Iran remains a popular destination, other countries like China, Nepal, Germany, Philippines, Ukraine, Bangladesh, and Kyrgyzstan also offer affordable courses for medical students. Despite the 5.8-year course duration, international students studying in Iran are exempted from the one-year preparatory course, adding to the overall appeal of pursuing MBBS in this dynamic and diverse country.

Why Choose Study MBBS in Iran 2024?

1. High-Quality Education:

7. Diverse Career Options:

Iran offers a wide range of career options for students based on their interests and aptitudes.

8. English Medium Instruction:

Cost Of Study MBBS In Iran

The cost of studying in Iran varies based on the student's preferences and the chosen university and degree. On average, the annual tuition fees for an MBBS program range from 6000 USD to 8000 USD, excluding living expenses.

1. Food And Accomodation

Accommodation costs in Iran range from 1500 USD to 2000 USD per year.

2. Living Costs

To live comfortably in Iran while studying, students should budget approximately 100 USD per month for living expenses, excluding tuition fees.

3. Travelling Costs

Public transportation in Iran is quite affordable. Students benefit from a 30% discount on regular train and bus tickets throughout the city. A one-way local transit ticket costs around 2 USD, while a monthly pass ranges from 10 USD to 30 USD.

Admission Process For MBBS In Iran 2024

The admission process for MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery) in Iran may vary slightly among different universities, but generally, the following steps are involved. Keep in mind that it's crucial to check the specific requirements of the university you are interested in, as they may have unique criteria.

    Research and Choose a University:
  1. Look for universities in Iran that offer MBBS programs. Some popular choices include Moscow State Medical University, Saint Petersburg State Medical University, and Kazan Federal University.
  2. Check the university's reputation, accreditation, and the medium of instruction (some programs are offered in English).
    3. Eligibility Criteria:
  3. Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria set by the chosen university. Typically, this includes a high school diploma or an equivalent qualification with a background in science (biology, chemistry, physics).
    4. Entrance Exams:
  4. Some universities may require you to take an entrance exam. The most common exam is the NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) for Indian students. Other universities may have their own entrance exams.
    5. Application Process:
  5. Fill out the application form provided by the university. This can often be done online through the university's official website.
  6. repare the required documents, which may include academic transcripts, a copy of your passport, passport-sized photographs, a medical certificate, and proof of language proficiency (if the program is in English).

Eligibility Criteria to Study MBBS in Iran
  1. The student must have a complete age of 17 years by 31st December of the admission year.
  2. The student must secure 50% marks in their 12th standard in Science stream (Physics, Chemistry and Biology) in CBSE / ISC or other Equivalent Board of Examination.
  3. Student must qualify the NEET exam
